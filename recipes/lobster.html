Recipe for Lobster

Steamed lobster tails with a hint of beer. Great with melted butter, lemon juice, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole lobster tails
  • 1/2 can beer

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, over medium to high heat, bring beer to a boil.
  2. Place a steamer basket on top of the saucepan; place thawed lobster tails in the basket and cover. Reduce heat and simmer for 8 minutes.
